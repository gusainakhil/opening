Our Story

Where Pahadi Soul Meets Every Occasion

Pahadi Basera is not just a venue — it is a living tribute to the spirit of Uttarakhand. Born from the mountains of Dehradun, we blend the warmth of Garhwali hospitality with modern elegance, offering you the most authentic Pahadi experience for every celebration.

From the earthy scent of pine to the melodious folk of Dhol-Damau, every corner of Pahadi Basera breathes the culture of the hills. Our chefs craft traditional recipes — Aloo Ke Gutke, Kafli, Chainsoo — using ingredients sourced straight from Himalayan farms.

  • Authentic Garhwali & Kumaoni Hospitality
  • Traditional Pahadi Cuisine & Modern Fine Dining
  • Scenic Mountain & Forest Views
  • Folk Music, Dance & Cultural Performances
